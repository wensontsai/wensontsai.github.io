sock full of nickels

23 September 2013

The dimensions of variables inside variables is increasing. Today, we worked on Rspec testing/coding a program that pits a player vs a dealer in blackjack with the ability to write/load different strategies for game play. It trucked along, the violence, and the bloodshed ~ they beat me over the head, they brutalize me! Complaints of police brutality? This is code brutality – growing pains still growing on day six. But, the tolerance for it has grown as well. It’s become less personal. The sailor begin to understand the waves. I went home around the usual dismissal for one of the first times since the course begun. Knowing your limits is vital for keeping on pace and riding the distance. Get, git, get ‘em tomorrow. Power posing in bathrooms across the nation!

death of pig-latin

28 September 2013

I thought it would take me the next 5 years of my life, but with a shit-ton of help and explanation, failed thought process/approaches, hipchat support, sacrifice of a saturday, housecat jealousy, pure grudgery sprinkled on top, I re-wrote the code for the pig-latin testfirst.org problem, got it to work, and also understand it.

Arrays to strings to arrays to arrays to strings to goddamnit computer, why cant you just think the way I think, and we can be friends and share the same underpants? You don’t wear underpants.

Pig-Latin is dead!!

5 years of life has come back to me, and now I’m thinking of skipping off to enjoy a Saturday night instead of working all the way through. I am not a computer – just yet.
